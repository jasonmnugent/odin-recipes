Chicken Parm

Home Jason's Egg Sammy Pulled Pork Sandwich

Chicken Parm is a classic meal that you can find in any Italian-American household. It is always a go-to comfort food for myself, and my recipe has made a name for itself amongst my family members.

The ingredients you will need are as follows:

  • chicken tenderloins
  • salt
  • pepper
  • flour
  • 1 egg
  • panko bread crumbs
  • olive oil
  • fresh mozzarella
  • fresh parmesan
  • fresh basil
  • marinara sauce
