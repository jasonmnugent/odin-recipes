Jason's Signature Egg Sammy

Home Chicken Parm Pulled Pork Sandwich

If you are living with me or happen to be at my place on any given morning, this is very likely what you will see me preparing. It is an easy, go-to meal that gives you the nutrients that you need to start your day.

Ingredients

  • one Dave's everything bagel
  • 2 eggs
  • everything but the bagel seasoning
  • ketchup
  • one avocado

Directions

  1. turn stove on high heat and crack 2 eggs onto the pan

  2. as egg white begins to cook, put the Dave's bagel in the toaster

  3. scramble eggs and cook until hard

  4. place eggs on toasted bagel

  5. slive the avocado and apply it to the sandwich

  6. apply ketchup to the sandwich

  7. enjoy!
